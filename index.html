In the software I created for the NBA, I generate predictions based on the last matches of the teams. "n" is the last match number. For example; For n=5, the last 5 matches of the teams are used in the match played. On a daily basis, there are 10 n's for each game. In other words; each mac has 10 predictions.

There are multiple macs in a day. The "n" numbers that hold the most for each day are drawn from the database and reflected as timeseries.
